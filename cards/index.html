Pokemon

Pikachu

An Electric type pokemon. It has Thunder, Thunder-Bolt, Electro Ball, Electro Web, Quick Attack, Volt Tackle, Iron Tail, Agility.
Evolutions: Pichu, Pikachu, Raichu, Alolan Raichu

Charizard

A Fire and Flying type pokemon. It has Flame Thrower, Fire Blast, Dragon Range, Dragon Tail, Quick Attack, Seismic Toss, Smoke, Steel Wing.
Evolutions: Charmander, Charmeleon, Charizard, Mega Charizard-X (or) Mega Charizard-Y

Greninja

A Water and Dark type pokemon. It has Aerial Ace, Water Shuriken, Double Team, Cut, Quick Attack, Water Pulse, Smoke Screen.
Evolutions: Froakie, Frogadier, Greninja
